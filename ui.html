Dashboard UI Projects

GrowthTrack: Marketing Analytics Dashboard

Dashboard / Data Visualization

The objective was to design 'GrowthTrack,' a clean and intuitive analytics dashboard for marketing professionals. The design focuses on presenting complex campaign data in a scannable and easy-to-understand format, allowing users to quickly assess performance and make data-driven decisions.

Design Highlights:
  • ✓ At-a-Glance KPI Cards: Key metrics like Reach, Engagement, and ROI are displayed prominently at the top, offering an immediate overview of campaign health.
  • ✓ Clear Data Visualizations: A performance line graph and a traffic source donut chart translate complex data into easily digestible visual trends and patterns.
  • ✓ Actionable Campaign Table: A detailed table of recent campaigns allows users to drill down into specific results, providing the granular data needed for strategic adjustments.

SocialPulse: Social Media Dashboard

Dashboard / SaaS UI

The goal was to design 'SocialPulse,' a user-friendly dashboard for social media managers. The UI needed to provide a clear overview of key performance metrics, enabling users to easily track growth, understand audience engagement, and manage their content strategy effectively.

Design Highlights:
  • ✓ Key Metric Overview: KPI cards at the top provide an instant summary of the most important stats, including trend indicators for quick performance assessment.
  • ✓ Visual Growth Tracking: The "Follower Growth" line graph offers a clear visual representation of audience trends over time, helping users understand their strategy's impact.
  • ✓ Content Strategy Insights: A "Content Distribution" donut chart gives a simple, at-a-glance view of the content mix, helping to analyze and balance posting strategies.
